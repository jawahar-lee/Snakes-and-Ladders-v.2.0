Gameplay:-

The players will move their pieces in the incremental order starting at 1, following the numbers on the board. If a player rolls a 4, then the player would move their piece four places. Click on the blackhole to roll the dice. If a player rolls 1 or 6, then he/she gets another chance to roll.


Landing on a snake's head:-

When a player's piece lands on a square that has a snake's head, then the snake swallows the piece and pulls it down to the square where it's tail is located.


Landing on the bottom of a ladder:-

When a player lands on a square that has the base of a ladder, their playing piece immediately climbs up to the square where the top of the ladder is located.


Winning the game:-

The first player to reach the highest number on the board(100), wins the game. To win, the player will need to roll the exact number to get to the last square. If the player rolls a higher number than needed to land exactly on 100, their piece does not move and remains there until their next turn.
